How Come Back Alive Helps

Our fund supports the Armed Forces of Ukraine through financing purely defence initiatives.
Since 2014 we have provided around 1000 thermal imagers and over 250 UAVs. In addition to
the material support, we increased technological capabilities of the Army through providing
1,500 tablets with Armor software aimed at stopping the artillery. Come Back Alive also
organizes trainings to equip our military with crucial skills. In 2015 we launched trainings for
medical workers, artillerists, snipers, minesweepers, and defence analysts. After coming back
from the front line, our military members have access to sport rehabilitation and veteran.
business opportunities.
Our organization does not use funds for purchasing arms. Our mission is limited to supplying
technology, trainings, and accouterments to help save lives of Ukrainians and help our warriors
defend Ukraine.
